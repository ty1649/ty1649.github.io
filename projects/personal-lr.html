3D Printable Recirculating Ball Linear Rails

Skills Gained

CAD FDM 3D Printing Compliant Mechanisms Rapid Prototyping/Iterations Tolerance Analysis Bearing Design Design for Manufacturing

Description

Situation/Task:

Wanted to custom design and manufacture accurate linear rails which are able to seta x amount of weight, while also moving accurately and smoothly.

Summary

After iterating 7-8 times, I developed a complete linear rail system that is all 3D printed, with only tiny bearing balls to be bought.

  • Created an adjustable preload mechanism with a compliant mechanism that can be tuned after assembly to optimize between smoothness and rigidity for accuracy and speed
  • Engineered precise ball return pathways to ensure smooth transition of bearing balls between loaded and return channels
  • Developed a modular design that allows for easy adjustment of rail length and block size
  • Implemented features to allow for easy printing and assembly

Result: Successfully created fully functional linear rails, used in my pen plotter project. Also taught me a lot about DFM (especially for 3d printing), the iterative process and problem solving, and helped me gain a deep understanding on how bearings/linear rails work.
