CV Boot Mold Design & Manufacturing

Skills Gained

Mold Design Design Iteration Cost Reduction Manufacturing Material Science

Problem Statement

As part of Bruin Formula Racing, I needed to design and manufacture custom CV boots to protect the constant velocity joints on our Formula SAE vehicle. The existing boots were dysfunctional, and commercial replacements were costly. The solution needed to be reliable, cost-effective, and manufacturable using our available resources.

My Work

Led the development through multiple design iterations:

  • Designed an innovative mold featuring a novel dovetail slider mechanism for efficient boot production
  • Iterated through 5 versions of the mold design, continuously improving functionality and ease of use
  • Created custom pouring processes and procedures for consistent boot production
  • Engineered the mold for easy release and minimal post-processing of finished boots
  • Developed quality control measures to ensure boots met performance requirements

Results

The CV boot project achieved several key improvements:

  • Successfully replaced dysfunctional CV boots with reliable custom-made alternatives
  • Reduced CV boot costs by 50% compared to commercial options
  • Created a repeatable manufacturing process through dovetail slider mechanism
  • Achieved consistent quality across multiple boot productions
  • Developed a solution that could be easily replicated by future team members

Lessons Learned

This project provided valuable insights into:

  • Importance of iterative design in complex mold development
  • Balance between material properties and manufacturing constraints
  • Cost optimization through in-house manufacturing
  • Value of innovative mechanical solutions like the dovetail slider
  • Documentation of manufacturing processes for knowledge transfer
