Hydrogen Fuel Cell

Skills Gained

CAD Modeling CFD Analysis Thermal Analysis CAM Programming DFM/DFA Project Management Technical Documentation Team Leadership

Description

Situation: As the Hydrogen Lead for the UCLA Supermileage Vehicle team, I was tasked with integrating a hydrogen fuel cell to work seamlessly with the car's systems to supply continuous power to the wheels.

Task: I led the initiative to design, analyze, and manufacture our own PEM open cathode fuel cell, ensuring it met the team's performance and integration requirements.

Summary

Action:

  • I divided the subteam into two specialized groups: MEA, focusing on chemistry and materials science, and Hardware, focusing on the mechanical components and analysis.
  • The MEA team collaborated with the Huang Lab to develop cost-effective membrane coating techniques.
  • The Hardware team designed and analyzed fuel cell components, considering factors like mass transfer, cooling, and clamping force.
  • I provided guidance on technical challenges, set the subteam's trajectory, and managed communication with sponsors and other teams.

Result:

  • We documented our research and design approach in a comprehensive slideshow and organized past designs and timelines in a Notion workspace.
  • The project enhanced my leadership skills and provided insights into team management, technical analysis, and cross-disciplinary collaboration.
