Self Balancing Robot

Skills Gained

Arduino Nano Soldering Product Design Surface Modeling

Problem Statement

Situation/Task: Building a cute compact self-balancing robot was the main goal, aiming to make it as small as possible.

Summary

Action: I engaged in circuit design, and applied circuit theory in real-life scenarios alongside short hand calculations to spec out motors and microcontrollers necessary. I also focused on the mounting and placement of electrical systems. Additionally, I explored control theory with PID control.

Result: Through this project, I gained valuable skills in Arduino Nano, soldering, product design, and surface modeling.
